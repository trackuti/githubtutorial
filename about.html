About Dusting Divas Cleaning company

At Dusting Divas We understand your home is important to you. That’s why we focus on the quality of the clean. Our cleaners Professional. They care as much as we do.

Our Staff at Dusting Divas Cleaning company

We really appreciate our employees. We have family fun days with our employees, kids, husbands, and boyfriends. We want to get to know their family.
Sometimes they receive up to 2 gift cards per month. We have them fill out their favorite restaurant or store they like to shop at. We buy gift cards from them. We make them spin the wheel to land on one of these gift cards. They love coming in early every day to see each other and talk about their personal lives. They always feel part of the family.
Our cleaners can see the comments of appreciation themself. We also give them a £10.00 bonus each from each client when their name is also mentioned on a Google review for the world to see.
